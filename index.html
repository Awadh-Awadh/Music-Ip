Who are Sauti Sol?

Bien-Aim√© Baraza, Willis Chimano, and Savara Mudigi founded the Kenyan afro-pop band Sauti Sol in Nairobi, Kenya in 2005. Polycarp Otieno, a guitarist, joined the group before they changed their name to Sauti.Mwanzo, Sauti Sol's debut studio album, was released on November 1, 2008, to critical acclaim. Sol Filosofia, their second studio album, was released on February 25, 2011, and received numerous accolades and nominations. The band collaborated with South African rapper and record producer Spoek Mathambo to release a self-titled extended play on June 18, 2012.
